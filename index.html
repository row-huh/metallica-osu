How to Play

  1. Press the Start button to begin the game
  2. Notes will fall down each lane following the "We Will Rock You" pattern
  3. Press the corresponding key (A, J) when a note reaches the white line
  4. Notes will disappear as soon as you press the key
  5. Perfect timing gives you more points!
  6. Build your combo for higher scores
